## BFE Pilot- und Demonstrationsprogramm

**URL:** https://www.bfe.admin.ch/bfe/de/home/forschung-und-cleantech/pilot-und-demonstrationsprogramm.html

**Overview:** Federal program for pilot and demonstration projects in energy research and innovation.

**Eligibility:**
- Innovative energy projects
- Swiss-based projects
- Technical or organizational innovation
- Demonstrable impact on energy transition

**Funding:**
- Pilot projects: up to CHF 200K (max 50% of costs)
- Demonstration projects: up to CHF 2M (max 40% of costs)
- Platform/software projects typically qualify as pilot

**Requirements:**
- Detailed project description
- Budget breakdown
- Timeline (12-36 months typical)
- Impact metrics
- Letter of intent from partners (municipalities, academic institutions)

**Application:**
- Rolling submissions
- Review takes 2-3 months
- Contact: pilot-demonstration@bfe.admin.ch

**Fit for OpenLEG:** ★★★★★
- Perfect fit: digital platform enabling energy communities
- Municipality partnerships demonstrate real-world impact
- Open-source model shows public benefit
- Target: CHF 50-100K for 18-month project

## EnergieSchweiz

**URL:** https://www.energieschweiz.ch

**Overview:** Federal program for voluntary energy efficiency and renewable energy projects.

**Relevant Programs:**
- "Digitale Energielösungen" (Digital Energy Solutions)
- "Energieeffizienz in Gemeinden" (Energy Efficiency in Municipalities)

**Funding:**
- Project support: CHF 10-50K
- Networking/events: smaller grants
- Co-financing model

**Requirements:**
- Alignment with EnergieSchweiz goals
- Measurable energy impact
- Dissemination plan

**Application:**
- Annual call (usually Q1)
- Next deadline: likely March 2027
- Contact via website form

**Fit for OpenLEG:** ★★★☆☆
- Smaller grants than Pilot program
- Municipality focus is good fit
- Digital solutions angle works
