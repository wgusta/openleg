## Innosuisse Innovation Cheque

**URL:** https://www.innosuisse.ch/inno/de/home/foerderinstrumente/fuer-kmu/innovationsscheck.html

**Overview:** CHF 15,000 voucher for SME-academic research partnerships

**Eligibility:**
- Swiss SME (or individual/startup)
- Academic partner (university, Fachhochschule)
- Defined research question
- 6-month project timeline

**Process:**
1. Find academic partner
2. Define research question jointly
3. Submit online application (simple)
4. Approval within weeks
5. Execute project
6. Academic institution invoices Innosuisse directly

**Research Questions for OpenLEG + ETH/ZHAW:**
1. "How can public energy data optimize LEG formation recommendations?"
2. "What clustering algorithms best identify potential LEG members?"
3. "How do Swiss municipalities evaluate LEG platform adoption?"

**Application:**
- Rolling submissions
- Very high approval rate
- No deadlines

**Next Steps:**
1. Contact ETH/ZHAW partner
2. Agree on research question
3. Submit Innovation Cheque application
4. Use funding for research assistant/data analysis

**Fit:** ★★★★★ (Easy, low-risk, builds academic relationship)

## Partnership Value Proposition

**What OpenLEG Offers to Academics:**

1. **Real-world data:** ElCom tariffs, Sonnendach solar, Energie Reporter, smart meter uploads
2. **Live platform:** Not a simulation - real municipalities, residents, LEG formations
3. **API access:** Public API for research, full database access for partners
4. **Policy relevance:** LEGs are new (Jan 2026), research is timely
5. **Publication opportunities:** Novel dataset, real implementation insights
6. **Thesis supervision support:** We can co-supervise with professors

**What We Need from Academics:**

1. **Validation:** Research validates our approach, algorithms, impact
2. **Students:** Thesis work extends platform capabilities
3. **Publications:** Academic papers give us credibility
4. **Network:** Access to energy policy/research community
5. **Grant support:** Academic partner strengthens BFE Pilot application
